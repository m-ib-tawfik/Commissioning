THE TRANSITION: RFSU & PSSR WORKFLOW

The shift from Pre-Commissioning to Operations requires strict procedural authorization.

RFSU: Technical Readiness Checklist

(Required for Contractor Handover)

  • All **Category A & B Punch Items** closed.
  • All **Functional Tests (FTs)** completed (signed off by Contractor/Company).
  • **Tightness Test** verified (e.g., $90\%$ Design Pressure).
  • **Inerting/Drying** complete (e.g., $<2.0\%$ O₂).
  • **Operational Inventory** (spares, fills) confirmed on-site.

PSSR: Safety Authorization Gate

(Mandatory before Raw Materials)

  • **PSSR Team** (Cross-Functional) mobilized and audit conducted.
  • **All PSVs/ESD systems** confirmed operational and sealed.
  • **Operating Manuals** reflect "as-built" reality.
  • **Personnel Training** records validated (required know-how confirmed).
  • **Temporary Bypasses** physically removed and normalized.

CONTRACTUAL TESTING: THE 72-HOUR PERFORMANCE TEST RUN

PTR STABILITY WINDOW (NEGATION RISK)

The chart shows the mandatory operating band for the 72-hour Maximum Rate Performance Test. Any deviation outside the $\pm 3\%$ window, even for a moment, results in a failure and test restart from zero hours.

TEST RUN DATA LOGGING

Data must be collected hourly for 72 hours:

  • Feedstock Composition & Quality
  • Utility Consumption (Power/Steam per unit)
  • Process Parameters (T, P, Flow)
  • Product Quality Analyses
⏱️ 12 HRS: Minimum Rate Test Duration

FINAL ACCEPTANCE: TRANSFER OF LIABILITY

The final sign-off transfers the legal responsibility (CCC) and triggers the end of the Contractor's execution phase.

Provisional Acceptance (PAC)

Issued after successful PTR.

  • **Transfer of CCC** is executed.
  • Contractor liability for construction ends (except warranty).
  • **Warranty Period** officially begins.

Final Acceptance (FAC)

Issued at end of Warranty Period.

  • All **Category C Punch Items** must be resolved.
  • Final documentation updates (As-Builts, O&M) are transferred.
  • **Contractor's liability** is fully discharged.
