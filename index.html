COMMISSIONING EXCELLENCE

Technical Handout Series (Day 1 - Day 4)

Select a day below to review the detailed methodology, critical checklists, and integrity verification protocols for each phase of the project lifecycle.

DAY 1

Foundations & Pre-Commissioning (P-Cx)

Focuses on the initial quality and integrity checks: Mechanical Completion (MC), Punch List categorization (A/B/C), **Pressure Testing** (Hydro/Pneumatic), and alignment checks.

  • Key Milestone: Mechanical Completion (MC).
  • Core Activity: Static checks and Cleaning Velocities (Air/Water).
  • Safety Focus: Stored Energy Hazard in Pneumatic Testing.
 DAY 2

Dynamic Verification & Operational Testing (Cx)

Covers the energized phase: **Live Loop Checking (LLC)**, Interlock and SIS testing, leak tightness, inerting, and operational runs for rotating equipment.

  • Key Milestone: Ready for Commissioning (RFC).
  • Core Activity: Functional Performance Tests (FPTs) and Dew Point Verification.
  • Safety Focus: Purging to achieve < 2.0% Oâ‚‚ and Preservation to maintain warranty.
 DAY 3

Initial Start-Up & Contractual Acceptance (PAC)

Details the transfer to live operations: passing the **PSSR**, controlled feed introduction, and the crucial 72-hour **Performance Test Run (PTR)** which triggers formal handover.

  • Key Milestone: Ready For Start-Up (RFSU) and Provisional Acceptance (PAC).
  • Core Activity: Performance testing (72-hour run) and $\mathbf{\pm 3\%}$ stability rule.
  • Safety Focus: PSSR (Pre-Start-up Safety Review) as the final gate.
 DAY 4

Utilities, Infrastructure & Specialized Systems

Focuses on critical support systems: Fire Pump validation (**NFPA 20**), HVAC balancing (TAB), power systems (UPS/Generator), and specialized industrial assets (Crude Oil Tanks/Offshore SPM).

  • Key Systems: Fire Protection, MV/LV Power, HVAC.
  • Core Activity: NFPA 20 pump curve verification.
  • Safety Focus: CAF/COF fluid hierarchy and Life Safety interlocks.
